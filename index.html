How Parents Can Support Healthy Smiles at Home

Good oral health starts long before your child walks into our office. Parents play an important role in daily dental care. Here are a few simple but effective tips:

  • Brush as soon as the first tooth appears
  • Use fluoride toothpaste (pea-sized for kids, smear for toddlers)
  • Introduce flossing when teeth begin to touch
  • Limit sticky, sugary snacks and sodas
  • Encourage water and healthy foods for strong teeth
  • Schedule dental check-ups every six months
